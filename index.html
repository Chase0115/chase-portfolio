About me

As a front-end developer with a passion for clean and efficient code, I have spent countless hours honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My goal is to create visually stunning and user-friendly websites that leave a lasting impression on those who use them.

I have experience working with popular front-end frameworks such as React.js and styled-components, as well as back-end technologies such as Node.js. In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on my ability to communicate effectively with clients and team members, ensuring that projects are completed on time and to the highest standard.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking in the mountains or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I am also an active member of the open-source community, contributing to a number of projects on GitHub.
